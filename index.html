About TechFlow Labs

TechFlow Labs LLC is a technology company specializing in developing AI-powered microservices and machine learning solutions for e-commerce platforms. Our core focus is building intelligent product discovery and recommendation systems that enhance online shopping experiences.

We help small to medium-sized e-commerce businesses implement AI-driven features to improve customer experience and increase conversion rates. Our solutions enable retailers to automate product recommendations and streamline the purchasing process.

Our technology stack includes Python, TensorFlow, Node.js microservices, and cloud infrastructure on AWS with containerized deployments.
